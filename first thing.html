What is MCL?

MCL is an online Minecraft community where we hold monthly tournaments in an arcade style fashion. It is usually played in 10 teams of 4 and it is all for fun. Teams are randomly set unless you specify who you want to be teamed with. We play 8 different gamemodes that are both team based and skill based. We also hold events for testing of something new and in development.

Our Discord

The MCL discord is where we announce upcoming projects and when the next tournament will be. Here you will be able to contact the creators of tournamnet and be able to talk to other community members. We also feature new updates that will be coming to the tournament such as new games, a map change, and how the tournament will go. Ranks

Ranks are Do Not give any advantage in the tournaments what so ever. They are purely for cosmetic purposes during the tournament. Of course, these cosmetics come at a cost. Ranks are a way for the community to support the creators and keep the tournaments going with an incentive given to the supporter. The rank will display a tag next to your in-game name during our events. Depending on the rank package you buy, more custommization will be available such as join/leave messages, your text color, tag color and much more.
